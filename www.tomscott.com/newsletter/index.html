Tom Scott's Weekly Newsletter

Want to see some good stuff from the internet? Every Monday, I send tens of thousands of people an email that contains anything I’ve made that week — but more importantly:

  • other good stuff I’ve found on YouTube, sometimes well-known, sometimes obscure
  • interesting links to articles and strange stuff I've found over the week
  • (very) occasionally, upcoming secret projects that need testing

This way, I can reach people even if The Algorithms disapprove. If that's for you, put your email address here!

You can unsubscribe at any time, and I won't pass your email address on to marketing jackals. See the full privacy policy and email provider AWeber's permission pledge.
